About Score Upgrades

The upgrade feature automatically adjusts scores within a specified range to the minimum passing mark of 50%. This applies to borderline cases where students are close to passing.

Scores between 45% and 49% will be adjusted to 50%
Example: 47% → 50%
Scores between 46% and 49% will be adjusted to 50%
Example: 48% → 50%
Scores between 47% and 49% will be adjusted to 50%
Example: 49% → 50%
Scores between 48% and 49% will be adjusted to 50%
Example: 48.5% → 50%
Only scores exactly at 49% will be adjusted to 50%
Example: 49% → 50%
No score adjustments will be applied. Only scores ≥50% will pass.
Example: 49.9% remains 49.9% (Failed)
